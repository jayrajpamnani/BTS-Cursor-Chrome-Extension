# Privacy Policy for BTS Cursor Extension

**Last updated:** [Current Date]

## Introduction

BTS Cursor is a Chrome extension that customizes your browser cursor with BTS logos. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Don't Collect:
- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Browsing history
- Website content
- User behavior data
- Analytics or tracking data

### What We Store Locally:
- Your cursor preference settings (auto/white/black)
- Whether the extension is enabled/disabled
- These settings are stored only in your browser's local storage

## Data Usage

### Local Storage:
- Cursor preferences are stored locally in your browser
- Settings sync across your Chrome profile (if sync is enabled)
- No external servers receive this data

### Extension Functionality:
- The extension injects CSS to change cursor appearance
- Background color detection happens locally in your browser
- No data is sent to external servers

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there's no data to secure. Your cursor preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the extension and notify users through the Chrome Web Store.

## Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer page.

## Compliance

This extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

## Data Retention

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data retention policy. Your local settings remain in your browser until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data.
